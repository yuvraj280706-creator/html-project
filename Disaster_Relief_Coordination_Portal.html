Safety Guidelines

  • Stay calm during emergencies.
  • Follow official evacuation orders.
  • Carry drinking water and basic medicines.
  • Keep emergency phone numbers accessible.
  • Do not spread unverified information.
  • Help elderly people, children and persons with disabilities.
  • Keep important documents in waterproof bags.
  • Avoid flooded roads and damaged buildings.
  • Listen to official weather updates.
  • Report emergencies immediately.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fast, reliable and coordinated disaster response, ensuring that every affected person receives shelter, food, clean water, medical assistance and timely rescue support.

Our Vision

To build resilient communities by improving disaster preparedness, encouraging volunteer participation and ensuring effective relief coordination through technology.
