Favourite Movie List

Movies are more than just entertainment for me. Some movies motivate me to work harder, some teach important life lessons, and some simply help me relax after a long day. Here are a few of my favorite movies and why I like them.

Movie: 3 Idiots

Genre: Comedy, Drama

Why I Like It:

This movie teaches students to follow their passion instead of running behind marks and competition. It has a perfect mix of humor, emotions, and motivation.

Movie 2: Dhurandhar

Genre: Action, Thriller

Why I Like It:

I like this movie because of its powerful action scenes, strong characters, and exciting storyline. It keeps the audience engaged from beginning to end.

Movie 3: Avengers: Endgame

Genre: Action, Superhero

Why I Like It:

This movie shows teamwork, sacrifice, and determination. Seeing all the heroes come together makes it one of the most exciting movies I have watched.

Movie 4: Movie 5: Chhichhore

Genre: Comedy, Drama

Why I Like It:

This movie reminds us that failure is not the end of life. It teaches important lessons about friendship, perseverance, and handling challenges positively.

Conclusion

Movies are not just entertainment for me, they are a source of motivation, learning, and inspiration. These are some of my favorite movies, and each one has taught me something valuable.
